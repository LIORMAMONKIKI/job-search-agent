### Which Bucket Favours Lior Most Strongly

**International — but conditionally.**

The international pool has more roles that are *structurally built for* Lior's exact profile: the Generative AI Associate + Creative Technologist cluster maps directly onto the combination of prompt engineering, visual direction, ComfyUI, and content strategy that Lior already holds. Israel has no equivalent title cluster at meaningful volume.

However, Israel wins on **friction-free access**. If Lior is Israel-based, local availability eliminates the 40% relocation filter that blocks most international applications, and the "trajectory" signal means a non-linear CV is less penalised.

**Practical read:** Israel is the lower-risk, shorter-cycle path to interviews. International is the higher-ceiling, better-fit path — but requires a stronger portfolio artefact to clear the brand signal barrier before applications convert.
