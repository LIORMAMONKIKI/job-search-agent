### Which Bucket Favours Lior Most

**Israel favours Lior more strongly right now.**

Three structural reasons:

1. **No visa drag.** International loses 34%+ of roles before Lior even reads the JD. Israel is 100% accessible.
2. **Trajectory > brand.** Lior's build-from story (analyst skills + AI tooling + creative direction, compounding quickly) is exactly what Israeli hiring managers are signalling they want. International employers filter on past employer names before evaluating trajectory.
3. **The gap to qualify is narrower.** In Israel, closing SQL signal + surfacing Tableau exposure moves Lior from Low to Medium match on the dominant analyst cluster. In international, that same effort still leaves Lior outside the pure-analytics majority (98 Data Analyst, 43 BA) *and* short of the engineering bar for Solutions Engineer titles.

International becomes the stronger bucket **after** Lior has one or two demonstrable agent workflow or LoRA projects live — that's what converts the 144 Medium international matches into genuine applications. Right now, the creative-AI stack differentiates but doesn't yet qualify at scale.
