### Which Bucket Favours Lior Most Strongly

**Israel — and it's not close.**

Three compounding reasons:

1. **No visa drag.** Every international application carries a hidden cost (sponsorship confirmation, visa timeline, employer risk aversion). Israel removes that entirely. Same effort buys more outcomes.

2. **Lior's creative-AI stack is a genuine local differentiator.** ComfyUI + LoRA + Prompt Engineering appears in fewer than 3% of Israeli roles — meaning the supply side is thin. Internationally, the same stack competes against a global pool of candidates who've been building in public for longer.

3. **Israel is *inventing* Lior's job title.** "Creative AI-Solutions Specialist" is a net-new Israeli category. Employers writing new JDs are more willing to shape a role around a non-standard candidate. That's Lior's structural advantage — arrive early to a title that doesn't have an established hiring template yet.

International is the right *medium-term* market (higher volume, higher ceiling for some titles), but the friction-to-signal ratio is worse right now.
